\section{Using the Archive and Replay}
\label{sec:archive-replay}

Each paper-facing number is bound to fixed schema, source, projection, feature-space crosswalk, probe, and retained-field ledgers.  The archived replay is identified by the DOI and SHA-256 in the page-one availability block.  DuckDB/PostgreSQL runs validate SQL constructability only and are ignored by collision and field-certificate counts.  A user reruns the audit by naming a vocabulary, inspecting collapsed unequal signatures, and either retaining separating fields or stating that the coarser scope is intentional.  Any changed source, schema, probe, or projection is a new denominator.
