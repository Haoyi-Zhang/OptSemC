\textbf{Pushdown is an operator family.} Predicate movement, aggregation, join, limit, and Top-N delegation are separate contracts, and placement separates local rewrite from source delegation.  \textbf{Plan evidence is not isomorphic.} Logical plans, estimates, runtime profiles, and stage graphs expose different observability and decision-time states.  \textbf{Decision time changes semantics.} Compile-time join selection, repartitioning, and runtime adaptation are distinct \cite{pavlo2017selfdriving,kraska2018learnedindex,krishnan2018sage,yang2022balsa,dutt2019selectivity,yang2020neurocard}.  \textbf{Materialization and reuse are guarded.} CTE reuse and materialization controls decide whether an intermediate is folded, materialized, or blocks pushdown.
